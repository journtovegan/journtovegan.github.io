About Me 🌱

Welcome! My name is Kylie, and my plant-based journey began five years ago. I've always loved nature and all things outdoor adventure, but for a long time, I didn't fully understand how my choices impacted the world around me. Through conversation with friends, I became curious about making more conscious lifestyle choices. I wanted low-fuss swaps with meaningful, positive implications. Along the way, I've learned a lot, and found easy alternatives that work better for me, and the planet. I've created this space to share resources with others who are starting or continuing their own vegan journey!
